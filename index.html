Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

01 Overview

At Pod, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data to provide a seamless group trip planning experience. By using the app, you consent to the practices described here.

02 Data Collection & Usage

Precise Location Data

We collect GPS coordinates to enable real-time map features and group tracking. This includes Background Location access, allowing your trip members to see your progress even when the app is minimized.

Account Identifiers

We use Firebase Authentication to collect your email address and name. This is required to sync your itineraries and identify you to your travel group.

User Generated Content

Destinations, notes, and favorite locations you save are stored in our Firebase Realtime Database to ensure all group members have the latest trip details.

03 Data Sharing

Your data is private. We do not sell your personal information. Your precise location and trip updates are only shared with users you have explicitly authorized by joining a shared "Trip." We utilize Google Firebase for secure data infrastructure.

04 Data Control & Deletion

You maintain full control over your data. You may delete your account and all associated trip data at any time via the in-app settings menu. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed from our Firebase production servers.
